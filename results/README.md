Paper-facing result bundles live here.

- `iros_rh1/`: Experiment 1 artifacts, including recomputed multiseed summaries, per-method trajectories, and final comparison plots.
- `iros_rh2/`: Experiment 2 multiseed bundle across all reported methods.
- `iros_ablation/20260217_085404_ablation/`: SPF ablation metrics and per-variant outputs used for the paper analysis.
- `plots/`: Curated PDF/PNG figures used in the README and paper-facing documentation. Provenance notes live in `plots/PLOT_SOURCES.md`.

Pointers:

- `iros_rh1/LATEST_RECOMPUTE_RUN.txt` identifies the canonical Experiment 1 recompute run.
- `iros_rh2/LATEST_RUN.txt` identifies the canonical Experiment 2 multiseed run.

Legacy and exploratory directories under `results/` are kept separately from these curated bundles and are not required to reproduce the paper tables in the README.
